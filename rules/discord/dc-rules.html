Goldnet - Discord Rules

Please follow these rules in the Goldnet official server!

1. No cussing
Avoid using any profane language.
2. No words above 18
Avoid using inappropriate language or content for minors.
3. No giving personal info
Do not share personal information in any of the channels.
4. No ads or invitations to other groups
Advertising or inviting others to external groups is not allowed.
5. Do not be racist
Any form of racism will not be tolerated.
6. No spamming
Avoid excessive messages, pictures, or anything that can be considered spamming.
7. No bullying
Bullying, harassment, or any kind of abusive behavior is strictly prohibited.
8. No bots
Do not use bots without permission from server admins.
9. No links
Links to external websites are not allowed unless authorized.
